Tech Community Calendar

Welcome to our comprehensive tech events calendar, your gateway to the vibrant Aberdeen tech community. This calendar showcases upcoming workshops, meetups, hackathons, and conferences designed to foster learning and collaboration.

Our events cater to all skill levels, from beginners taking their first steps in technology to seasoned professionals looking to expand their expertise. Each event is carefully curated to provide maximum value and practical insights.

What to Expect

Events typically include hands-on workshops where you can develop new skills, networking sessions to connect with like-minded professionals, and talks from industry experts sharing cutting-edge developments.

The Aberdeen tech community prides itself on being inclusive and supportive. Whether you're interested in data science, web development, cybersecurity, or emerging technologies, you'll find events that match your interests.

Getting Involved

Participation is easy! Simply click on any event in the calendar to see details including location, time, and registration information. Most events offer both in-person and virtual attendance options to accommodate different preferences and situations.

We encourage community members to propose and organize events. If you have expertise to share or a topic you'd like to see covered, please reach out through our discussion forum.

Stay Updated

The calendar is updated regularly with new events. You can subscribe to receive notifications about upcoming events that match your interests or add this calendar to your personal calendar application.

Join us in building a thriving tech ecosystem in Aberdeen through knowledge sharing, collaboration, and community engagement.
